# Breathwork

## Vision
Develop a grounded, embodied breathwork practice that supports healing, clarity, leadership, and safe facilitation.

## Why This Matters
Breathwork is an important area of focus in my life, both as a personal practice and as something I have studied more deeply through the lightFORCE Academy with my teacher and friend Jeff Sorenson.

## Current Context
- Completed a year-long lightFORCE Academy container
- Trained in breathwork practice and facilitation
- Learned in relationship, mentorship, and community rather than through a shallow certificate-only approach

## What I Value In This Work
- Embodiment over performance
- Leadership over image
- Safety and integrity in facilitation
- Real transformation rather than chasing peak experiences
- Breath as both a ceremonial and everyday practice

## Ongoing Focus
- Deepen my own breathwork practice
- Integrate what I learned into daily life
- Continue learning how breath affects the nervous system, emotions, and state
- Keep refining what safe, grounded facilitation means
