# Health & Fitness

## Vision
Build a strong, resilient, pain-free body that supports energy, longevity, clear thinking, and sustainable performance.

## Current Focus
- Functional Patterns 10-week online program
- Improve movement quality, posture, and daily body awareness
- Reduce pain and stiffness from sitting and computer work
- Support long-term sustainable Jiu Jitsu training

## Priorities
- Move more freely and pain free
- Build a body that can handle both desk work and athletic practice
- Improve consistency more than intensity
- Prevent avoidable injuries through better movement patterns

## Practices
- Daily movement / mobility work
- Awareness of sitting posture and work habits
- Strengthening better movement mechanics
- Recovery habits that support training longevity

## Notes
- Web development work means long periods of sitting, which can lead to stiffness and pain when movement habits slip.
- Functional Patterns and Jiu Jitsu support each other well when approached sustainably.
- The goal is not just performance, but long-term freedom of movement and injury resilience.
