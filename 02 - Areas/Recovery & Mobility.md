# Recovery & Mobility

## Vision
Maintain a body that feels open, capable, pain-free, and ready for both focused work and athletic practice.

## Current Focus
- Reduce stiffness from sitting
- Improve movement quality and body awareness
- Support recovery between work and training
- Build sustainable habits that protect the lower back and overall posture

## Priorities
- Consistent movement over perfection
- Better recovery after training and long work sessions
- Injury prevention through awareness and mechanics
- Restore freedom and comfort in the body

## Inputs
- Functional Patterns practice
- Walking and regular position changes
- Mobility / recovery work
- Attention to lower back health

## Notes
- This area supports both day-to-day comfort and long-term athletic sustainability.
- Especially important because desk work can quietly build stiffness and compensation patterns over time.
