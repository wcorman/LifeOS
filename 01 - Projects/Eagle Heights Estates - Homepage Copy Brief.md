# Eagle Heights Estates - Homepage Copy Brief

## Purpose
Give the homepage enough copy structure that design and development can move faster, even before final polished copy is approved.

## Copy Direction
- Premium but approachable
- Clear, not overly salesy
- Trustworthy and grounded
- Aspirational without sounding generic
- Focused on clarity, lifestyle, and confidence

## Hero
### Copy prompts
- What is Eagle Heights Estates in one sentence?
- Why is it compelling?
- What action should the visitor take next?

### Starter copy direction
**Headline options:**
- Elevated living at Eagle Heights Estates
- Discover life at Eagle Heights Estates
- A premium place to live, build, and belong

**Subheadline direction:**
A short sentence describing the offering, the setting, and the opportunity in a way that feels confident and clear.

**CTA options:**
- Inquire Now
- View Opportunities
- Explore Eagle Heights Estates

## Intro / Overview
### Copy prompts
- What is the core opportunity here?
- Who is this for?
- What makes it feel different?

### Starter copy direction
A short paragraph introducing Eagle Heights Estates as a distinctive place defined by quality, setting, and long-term appeal.

## Why Eagle Heights Estates
### Copy prompts
- What are the top 3 to 5 reasons someone would choose this?
- What makes it different from alternatives?

### Starter pillar ideas
- Exceptional setting
- Thoughtful development
- Lifestyle and community appeal
- Long-term value
- Quality and trust

## Featured Properties / Opportunity
### Copy prompts
- What is actually available?
- What level of detail does a visitor need before inquiring?
- What makes the offering attractive right now?

### Starter copy direction
Short listing intros that emphasize clarity, desirability, and the ease of taking the next step.

## Lifestyle / Community
### Copy prompts
- What does life here feel like?
- What nearby advantages matter most?
- What emotional picture should the site create?

### Starter copy direction
Use a few short, image-supported blocks that help visitors imagine daily life, not just square footage or land value.

## Trust / Credibility
### Copy prompts
- Why should someone trust this project?
- What reduces hesitation?
- What proof or reassurance is available?

### Starter copy direction
A concise section that reinforces professionalism, care, legitimacy, and confidence in the opportunity.

## FAQ Preview
### Suggested starter questions
- What is Eagle Heights Estates?
- What opportunities are currently available?
- How do I inquire or take the next step?
- What makes this community unique?

## Final CTA
### Copy prompts
- What should visitors do when they are interested?
- What reassurance helps them act?

### Starter copy direction
Close with a clear invitation to inquire, paired with language that makes the next step feel easy and worthwhile.

## Notes
- This is not meant to be final approved copy.
- It is a writing and design scaffold so the site can move forward faster.
- Once real client messaging arrives, this note can guide refinement rather than starting from scratch.
