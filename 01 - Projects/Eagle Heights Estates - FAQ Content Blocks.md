## Block 3 - General FAQ
**Question 1:** What is Eagle Heights Estates?
**Answer 1:** Eagle Heights Estates is a residential acreage development offering large estate lots for sale just outside Saskatoon.

**Question 2:** Where is Eagle Heights Estates located?
**Answer 2:** Eagle Heights Estates is located near Saskatoon, offering buyers a quieter acreage lifestyle while keeping the city within easy reach.

**Question 3:** Who is Eagle Heights Estates designed for?
**Answer 3:** It is a strong fit for families looking for more space, buyers who want greater privacy, and people interested in building a custom home outside the city without moving far away from it.

## Block 4 - Lots / Opportunity FAQ
**Question 1:** What is being sold right now?
**Answer 1:** Individual estate lots are currently being offered for sale.

**Question 2:** Can I build a custom home on my lot?
**Answer 2:** Yes, buyers have the opportunity to purchase a lot and build a custom home on their land.

**Question 3:** Are all lots currently available?
**Answer 3:** Availability may change over time, so the best way to confirm current lot status is to request information directly.

**Question 4:** How can I explore lot options?
**Answer 4:** Visitors can review available listings and use the interactive lot map to better understand lot locations and opportunities within the development.

## Block 5 - Process FAQ
**Question 1:** How do I get more information?
**Answer 1:** The easiest next step is to request information through the contact page or inquiry form.

**Question 2:** Do I need to know which lot I want before reaching out?
**Answer 2:** No. You can reach out whether you already have a lot in mind or are still exploring your options.

**Question 3:** What happens after I inquire?
**Answer 3:** You will be contacted with more information and can continue the conversation based on your questions, level of interest, and what opportunities are currently available.
