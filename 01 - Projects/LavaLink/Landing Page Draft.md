# Landing Page Draft

## Working Title
Access Your Obsidian Vault From Any Browser

## Hero
### H1
Access your Obsidian vault from any browser.

### Subhead
LavaLink makes it possible to open and use your Obsidian vault online without forcing you to switch to a completely different notes app.

### CTA Ideas
- Get early access
- See how it works
- Access Obsidian in your browser

## Problem Section
A lot of Obsidian users run into the same problem:
- they want access on a work computer
- they need remote access from another machine
- they cannot install the Obsidian app everywhere
- they want browser access without leaving their existing vault behind

## Solution Section
LavaLink is built for people who already like Obsidian and want a browser-based way to access their vault.

Instead of replacing your workflow, the goal is to make your vault available where and when you need it.

## Benefit Ideas
- Access your Obsidian vault online
- Use Obsidian from a browser when the app is not available
- Keep working with your existing vault instead of migrating to a new tool
- Make remote access simpler and more direct

## FAQ Angles
### Is there a web version of Obsidian?
Not officially in the same way people expect from fully browser-native apps. LavaLink is aimed at solving that gap by making your vault accessible from the browser.

### Can I use Obsidian on a work computer?
If you cannot install the app on a work machine, browser access becomes the key requirement. LavaLink is positioned around exactly that use case.

### Why not just switch to another notes app?
Because many people do not want a different app. They want access to their existing Obsidian workflow and vault.
