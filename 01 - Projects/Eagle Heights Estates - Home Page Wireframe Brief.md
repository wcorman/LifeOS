# Eagle Heights Estates - Home Page Wireframe Brief

## Goal
Design a homepage that immediately feels trustworthy, premium, and clear, while guiding visitors toward inquiry.

## Overall Job Of The Page
- Communicate what Eagle Heights Estates is
- Create desire and trust quickly
- Show the lifestyle and opportunity
- Make the next step obvious

## Suggested Section Order

### 1. Hero
**Purpose:** Make a strong first impression and explain the core offer fast.

**Include:**
- Strong headline
- Short supporting subheadline
- Primary CTA
- Secondary CTA if useful
- High-quality visual background or hero image

**Questions to answer:**
- What is Eagle Heights Estates?
- Why should someone care right away?
- What should they do next?

### 2. Intro / Overview
**Purpose:** Give a concise overview of the development / offering.

**Include:**
- Short intro paragraph
- 2 to 4 key highlights
- Optional stat or quick trust point

### 3. Why Eagle Heights Estates
**Purpose:** Explain the core differentiators.

**Include:**
- 3 to 5 value pillars
- Brief supporting descriptions
- Simple icon or visual support if helpful

**Possible themes:**
- location
- quality
- lifestyle
- exclusivity
- long-term value

### 4. Featured Properties / Opportunity
**Purpose:** Bring the offering into focus and help users picture what is available.

**Include:**
- Featured listings or featured opportunities
- Key property details
- CTA to browse or inquire

### 5. Lifestyle / Community Section
**Purpose:** Sell the experience, not just the property.

**Include:**
- Short lifestyle-oriented intro
- Supporting imagery
- Key local / community benefits
- Emotional positioning around the lifestyle

### 6. Trust / Credibility Section
**Purpose:** Reduce hesitation and build confidence.

**Include:**
- Short trust statement
- Developer / brand credibility
- Optional testimonials, proof points, or process reassurance

### 7. Gallery Preview
**Purpose:** Let visuals create emotional momentum.

**Include:**
- Curated image preview
- Link to full gallery if one exists

### 8. FAQ Preview or Buyer Questions
**Purpose:** Remove common friction before inquiry.

**Include:**
- 3 to 5 key questions
- Link to full FAQ if needed

### 9. Final CTA / Inquiry Section
**Purpose:** End with a clear next step.

**Include:**
- Strong closing headline
- Brief reassurance
- Inquiry CTA or embedded form
- Contact info if appropriate

## Tone / Design Direction
- Premium but grounded
- Clean and modern
- Trustworthy and calm
- Spacious and image-led
- More clarity than hype

## Content Priorities
If content is limited, prioritize:
1. Strong hero copy
2. Clear overview
3. Real imagery
4. Value propositions
5. Clear CTA

## Build Notes
- The homepage should do the heaviest trust-building work.
- Keep sections focused and not overly dense.
- Every major section should support either clarity, desire, or conversion.
- Responsive layout matters a lot because mobile visitors may be primary.
