# Eagle Heights Estates - Client Questions & Missing Assets

## Questions For Client
- What is the final page / section scope?
- What content is already written?
- What content still needs to be written or refined?
- What branding assets are available?
- Are there logos, colors, fonts, or brand guidelines?
- What imagery is available?
- Are there property photos, lifestyle photos, drone shots, or renderings?
- What is the primary call to action?
- What should users do on the site, inquire, call, book, browse listings?
- Are there listings that need to be managed dynamically?
- Is a CMS required?
- What is the ideal launch date?
- Who gives final approval?

## Assets To Gather
- Logo files
- Brand colors / fonts
- Copy / messaging
- Property imagery
- Community / location imagery
- Testimonials if available
- Contact info
- Social links if relevant
- Domain / hosting / access details

## Clarifications To Protect Timeline
- What is in scope for version one?
- What can wait until after launch if needed?
- How many revision rounds are expected?
- How quickly can feedback be turned around?

## Notes
- Fast asset gathering will make or break a 3-week delivery timeline.
- Ambiguity early becomes stress later.
