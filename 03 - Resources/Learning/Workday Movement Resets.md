# Workday Movement Resets

## Purpose
Interrupt stiffness and low-quality posture from long periods of sitting and computer work.

## What This Note Is For
A simple menu of movement resets to use during the workday so the body does not drift too far into tension and compression.

## Reset Ideas
- Stand up and walk for a few minutes
- Change position before stiffness builds too much
- Breathe more fully and reset posture
- Do a short mobility sequence
- Unwind neck / shoulders / hips after long computer blocks

## Prompts
- How does my body feel right now?
- Where am I holding unnecessary tension?
- Have I been sitting too long?
- What is the smallest useful reset I can do right now?

## Good Times to Reset
- Between meetings
- After long focus blocks
- Before or after training
- When lower back, hips, or shoulders feel stiff
- When energy drops and posture collapses

## Notes
- The goal is not perfection, just regular interruption of bad patterns.
- Small resets done consistently may matter more than occasional big efforts.
