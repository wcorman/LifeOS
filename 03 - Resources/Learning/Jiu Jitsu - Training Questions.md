# Jiu Jitsu - Training Questions

## Current Questions
- What is my highest-percentage route to the back?
- Why do I lose control after getting to the back?
- What are the best finishing systems from the back for my current level?
- Which escapes should become automatic first?
- Which takedowns best fit my body and instincts?
- How do I stay calm when I get caught under pressure?
- How do I train more consistently without overdoing it?

## Questions After Sparring
- Where did I get stuck?
- Where did I panic?
- What position kept showing up?
- What opportunity did I miss?
- Was I too tense or too passive?
- Did I rely too much on my arms?
- What is one thing to study before the next session?

## Questions for Coaches / Training Partners
- What details am I missing on my back control?
- Why is my bow and arrow choke failing?
- What escape should I prioritize first from bottom?
- What takedown would you recommend I build around?
- Where am I wasting energy?
