# lightFORCE Academy

## My Experience
I went through a year-long lightFORCE Academy container with my teacher and friend Jeff Sorenson.

This training was important not just because of the content, but because of the container: a longer initiation into breathwork practice, facilitation, embodiment, leadership, and personal transformation.

## Important Clarification
The current public-facing lightFORCE Academy program appears somewhat different from the version I went through.

What seems aligned:
- breathwork
- embodied leadership
- deep mentorship / container work
- transformation through practice and lived experience

What is different:
- the website now speaks more explicitly about breathwork, cold exposure, and plant medicine across 1-year and 3-year pathways
- in the version I went through, there was not extensive training on using plant medicine
- my experience should not be treated as identical to the current program page

## What I Learned / Took From It
- Real facilitation is about much more than technique
- Embodiment matters more than performance
- Leadership means how you show up, not just what you know
- Transformation requires integration, not just intense experiences
- A strong container, mentorship, and community matter deeply

## Themes Reflected On The Current Page
Based on the current page, the Academy emphasizes:
- embodiment over superficial certification
- leadership over status
- deep mentorship and accountability
- small-group container work
- nervous system regulation
- ceremonial leadership
- transformation through practice, community, and immersion

## Notes On Jeff Sorenson
Jeff is my teacher and friend, and this training is part of why breathwork and facilitation matter to me in a deeper way.
