# Breathwork

## Why This Matters
Breath is both a daily practice and a profound tool for transformation.

What matters most is not just the big experience, but how breath changes state, awareness, and regulation in ordinary life.

## Current Context
- Studied breathwork through the lightFORCE Academy with Jeff Sorenson
- Learned breathwork in a deeper container focused on practice, embodiment, leadership, and facilitation
- Want to continue integrating breath as both a personal and relational practice

## What Stands Out
- Breath is foundational, not secondary
- Breath influences nervous system state in real time
- Breathwork is not only about altered states, but about moment-to-moment awareness and regulation
- Safe facilitation requires more than technique
- Embodiment and integrity matter deeply

## Areas of Interest
- Daily breath practice
- Nervous system regulation
- Emotional state shifts
- Breathwork facilitation
- Holding safe containers
- Integration after powerful experiences

## Questions to Explore
- What kinds of breath practices are most supportive in daily life?
- What makes someone truly safe and skillful as a facilitator?
- How do I integrate transformational experiences into ordinary living?
- How does breathwork intersect with spirituality, leadership, and embodiment?
