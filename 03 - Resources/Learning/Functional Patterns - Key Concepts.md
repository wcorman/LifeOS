# Functional Patterns - Key Concepts

## Concepts to Understand Better
- Posture
- Gait
- Fascial system
- Tension and compensation
- Rotation and contralateral movement
- Breathing and body mechanics
- How chronic sitting changes movement patterns

## What to Look For
- Which concepts actually change how I move?
- Which ideas reduce pain or stiffness?
- Which ideas seem foundational versus secondary?
- What carries over into work posture, walking, and Jiu Jitsu?

## Practical Filter
A concept is especially valuable if it helps with:
- reducing pain
- improving posture
- making movement feel more natural
- improving athletic sustainability
- increasing body awareness in daily life
