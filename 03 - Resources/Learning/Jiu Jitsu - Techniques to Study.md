# Jiu Jitsu - Techniques to Study

## Back Attacks
- Bow and arrow choke
- Rear naked choke finishing details
- Collar choke variations from the back
- Transitions between back finishes
- Maintaining back control while the opponent defends

## Escapes
- Mount escapes
- Side control escapes
- Back escape fundamentals
- Armbar defense and late escape awareness
- Headlock escape concepts

## Takedowns / Standing
- Uchi Mata mechanics and common counters
- Grip-fighting sequences
- Safer takedown entries
- Follow-ups when initial entries fail
- Takedowns that fit my body and timing

## Pressure / Control
- Using the legs for pressure and control
- Better wedge and frame awareness
- Controlling without burning out the arms
- Staying heavy and efficient rather than tense

## Questions While Studying
- What problem does this technique solve?
- What are the most common counters?
- What are the key details that make it work?
- Where does it fit into my actual game?
- Can I hit it live, not just in drilling?
