# Functional Patterns

## Why This Matters
I want to move more freely and pain free in my body.

As a web developer, I sit a lot during the day, which can lead to pain, stiffness, and compensation patterns when I am not mindful. Functional Patterns feels exciting because it offers a way to improve how I move, understand my body better, and build a more sustainable physical foundation.

## Current Context
- Started the Functional Patterns 10-week online program
- Motivated to put the work into practice, not just consume the ideas
- Interested in learning more about movement, posture, fascia, and long-term body mechanics

## What I Want From This
- Less pain and stiffness
- Better posture and movement quality
- More body awareness during work and daily life
- Better resilience for Jiu Jitsu and other physical training
- A body that feels integrated, functional, and sustainable over the long term

- Movement patterns

## Practical Applications
- Better workday movement habits
- Better recovery and mobility
- Lower injury risk
- Better athletic longevity
- Better integration between training and daily life
